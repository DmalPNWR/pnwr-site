About Pacific Northwest Report

Seahawks blog · built and written from the Canadian side of the PNW.

Pacific Northwest Report (PNWR) is a Seahawks-only blog run by a Canadian fan who happens to live in the Pacific Northwest North.

This isn’t “objective national coverage.” It’s the kind of obsessively detailed, emotionally irrational, deeply hopeful writing that only comes from someone who cares way too much about third-down conversion rates and who still thinks about the 2013 defense on a weekly basis.

On PNWR you’ll find:

  • Game previews and recaps that actually talk about scheme and matchups.
  • Player spotlights on current and emerging Seahawks.
  • Big-picture thoughts on where the team — and the franchise — is headed.

If you’re a Seahawks fan who lives in the PNW or just loves this team from afar, welcome. You’re in the right place.
